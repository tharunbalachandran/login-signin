Service

The Aadugalam website aims to provide a simple, efficient, and user-friendly platform for booking turf facilities for sports. The Turf Booking Service will allow users to reserve turf spaces for football, cricket, or other sports with ease. The goal is to create a hassle-free experience for users, enabling them to book sports facilities based on their preferences and schedule.

Features of the Turf Booking Service:

User Registration & Authentication:

Users can sign up or log in using email, phone number, or social media accounts. Profiles will store booking history, preferences, and favorite turfs.

Search & Filter Turf Options:

Users can search for turfs in specific locations (city, neighborhood).

Filters based on the type of sport (football, cricket, etc.), turf size (5-a-side, 11-a-side), availability, price, and amenities (lighting, locker rooms, etc.).

Live Turf Availability:

Real-time availability of turf slots. Users can view available time slots for booking and see if a turf is already reserved.

Booking & Reservation:

Simple and intuitive turf booking process.

Users can book turfs for specific dates and times.

Option for hourly or multiple-hour bookings.

Pricing displayed based on the duration, turf type, and peak/non-peak times.



Payment Gateway Integration:

Secure payment options (credit/debit cards, UPI, wallets).

Payment confirmation and invoice generation.

Option to apply discounts or promo codes.

Booking Confirmation & Notifications:

Instant email or SMS booking confirmation.

Reminders and updates about upcoming bookings.

Option to cancel or reschedule bookings within a certain time frame.



Rating & Reviews:

Users can rate turfs based on their experience.

Leave feedback about turf conditions, customer service, etc.



Admin Panel (for turf managers):

Turf managers can view and manage bookings. Option to update availability, pricing, and turf details. Manage user feedback and resolve issues.

Customer Support:

24/7 chat support or helpdesk integration for user queries. FAQs and self-service help.

Additional Services:

Option for additional services like equipment rental (e.g., balls, kits), coaching sessions, or catering for events. Event booking for tournaments or group activities.

User Flow:

Landing Page:

User visits the homepage of Aadugalam. Prominent call-to-action (CTA) buttons for "Book Turf" or "Find Turf."

Search & Select Turf:

User enters desired location, sport, and date range. Filter results by available times, turf size, and pricing. View turf options on a map or in a list with photos and descriptions.

Select Time Slot:

User selects a turf and then picks a time slot. A calendar view shows available dates and times.

Booking & Payment:

User provides necessary details (name, email, phone number). Payment screen appears to confirm booking with various payment methods.

Confirmation:

After successful payment, the user receives an email/SMS confirmation with booking details.

Manage Booking:

The user can view, modify, or cancel their booking in the "My Bookings" section.
